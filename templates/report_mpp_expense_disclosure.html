1. Split travel from hospitality

These figures are claim categories — travel, accommodation, meals, hospitality — not full office or staff budgets. Across the House, hospitality is about three-fifths of disclosed dollars. That is the discretionary pile worth ranking.

Travel-heavy
Northern and distant ridings: large travel, modest hospitality. Expect this. Geography is not a scandal.
Examples
Sol Mamakwa (Kiiwetinoong), Greg Rickford (Kenora—Rainy River), Guy Bourgouin (Mushkegowuk—James Bay).
Hospitality-heavy
Totals driven by hospitality — often GTA / 905 — with travel a smaller share. This is the accountability hook.
Examples
Hardeep Singh Grewal, Sheref Sabawy, Zee Hamid, Aris Babikian, Rudy Cuzzetto, Paul Calandra, Caroline Mulroney.

2. Caucus medians are close — composition is not

Total medians by party sit in a similar band. The sharper signal is who concentrates spend in hospitality, not which caucus “spends more” overall.

5. Methodology

  • Source pages: ola.org/en/members/expense-disclosure/list and each member’s claim table (travel, accommodation, meals, hospitality).
  • Window: roughly the past two years of published claims; refresh date shown above.
  • Pull & storage live in the OAC / ONAC MPP tracker — ProtectOnt publishes a synced snapshot (no second scrape).
  • Not included: full members’ office expenditures PDFs, staff salaries, or Premier claims when OLA has none in this window.
