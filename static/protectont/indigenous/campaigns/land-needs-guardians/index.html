Land Needs Guardians

Multiple Nations

National campaign for sustained federal investment in Indigenous Guardians programs and Indigenous-led conservation.

Official campaign site ↗

Why it matters

Indigenous Guardians are on-the-ground stewards of lands and waters — monitoring, protecting, and managing territory under Indigenous law. Sustained funding is essential for IPCAs and Land Back to succeed.

Background

Land Needs Guardians is a campaign calling on Canada to make major ongoing investments in Guardians programs and Indigenous-led conservation, including IPCAs.
