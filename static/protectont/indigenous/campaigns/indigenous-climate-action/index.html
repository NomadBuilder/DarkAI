Indigenous Climate Action

Multiple Nations

Indigenous-led climate justice organization connecting land defence, sovereignty, and climate policy across Turtle Island.

Official campaign site ↗

Why it matters

Indigenous Peoples are among the first and hardest hit by climate change — and lead many of the most effective resistance movements. ICA connects grassroots defence with policy and education.

Background

Indigenous Climate Action supports Indigenous-led climate justice through education, organizing, and amplifying frontline voices.
