Why it matters

Grassy Narrows has fought mercury contamination for generations. The community maintains one of the longest-running land blockades in Canadian history while pursuing legal action against Canada and Ontario.

Background

Industrial mercury dumping poisoned the English-Wabigoon river system. Grassy Narrows continues to demand compensation, river cleanup, and an end to industrial threats — including upstream mining and nuclear waste proposals.

Legal action proceeds with RAVEN and community support. Mercury care home construction began; industrial threats at headwaters remain a live concern.
