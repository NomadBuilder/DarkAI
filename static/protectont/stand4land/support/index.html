How you can help

The most effective support goes directly to Nations and campaigns — not through third-party fundraisers. We never collect donations on behalf of movements listed here.

Donate through official channels

Each campaign page lists verified donation links — legal funds, Land Back trusts, and frontline camp costs. If a link is not on the official campaign website or their verified social accounts, do not trust it.

Buy official merchandise

Some campaigns sell merch to fund their work. Purchase only from links on official campaign or Nation websites.

Volunteer & show up

Rallies, blockades, and community gatherings are announced on official campaign sites and Nation channels. Follow Indigenous-led safety guidance and respect protocols on the land.

Contact elected representatives

Pressure on provincial and federal governments matters — but centre Indigenous voices. Share campaign materials from official sources; do not speak over Nations.

Learn & share responsibly

Use our learning centre to understand Land Back, FPIC, and treaties — then share links to official campaign pages, not reworded summaries that erase Nation leadership.
