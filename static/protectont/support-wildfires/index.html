The first $250 is matched

To help get the campaign started, Aazir is personally matching the first $250 donated by the Protect Ontario community, dollar-for-dollar.

This is one combined matching pool. The maximum personal matching contribution is $250 in total, not $250 for each donor.

  • If the community donates $100, Aazir contributes $100.
  • If the community donates $250, Aazir contributes the full $250.
  • If the community donates $1,000, Aazir still contributes $250, bringing the combined total to $1,250.
