Take Action

What you can do

Contact your MPP, sign petitions, join protests, and stay informed. Public pressure works — the Greenbelt reversal proved it.

1

Contact your MPP

Tell your provincial representative you want accountability: strong public healthcare, protected water, species and Indigenous rights, and no favours for developers.

2

Sign petitions

Ontario Nature and the David Suzuki Foundation are calling on Premier Ford to cancel Bill 5 and protect species and Indigenous rights. Add your name.

  • Ontario Nature: Bill 5 — A moment to mobilize →
  • David Suzuki Foundation: Repeal Bill 5 →
3

Join protests and rallies

Anti-Ford and provincial accountability protests happen across Ontario — in Toronto, Ottawa, and other cities. Showing up in person makes the demand visible and harder to ignore.

  • View listed protests & rallies →
  • Search for Ontario protests and rallies (e.g. healthcare, Greenbelt, Bill 5) →
  • OFL Ford Tracker — campaigns and actions →
4

Stay informed and share

Use the data and sources on this site to back up what you say. Share Protect Ontario with others so more people see the pattern.

The Greenbelt reversal happened because people showed up, wrote, and refused to accept that protected land was for sale. The same kind of pressure can protect healthcare, water, species, and Indigenous rights.
