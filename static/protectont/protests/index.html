First time?

Protest FAQ

Nervous about what to expect? You're not alone. Here are straightforward answers for people heading to their first rally—especially upcoming province-wide days.

Yes. Many people at province-wide days are attending their first rally. You don't need experience—showing up with a sign or simply standing in solidarity counts. Arrive a few minutes early, find the gathering point listed for your city, and follow what the crowd and any marshals or organizers are doing.

Why this page exists

Ontario has no one official protest calendar. Actions are announced by unions, community groups, health coalitions, and local organizers—often on social media, email lists, or separate websites. Protect Ontario gathers upcoming dates here so you can see what's happening near you without hunting across platforms.

Listings focus on actions tied to public services, environmental protection, land use, and provincial accountability—the same themes covered elsewhere on this site. We verify links when we can; always confirm time and location with the organizer before you travel.
